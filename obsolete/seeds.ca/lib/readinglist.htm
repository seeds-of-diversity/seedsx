published by Owl Books, 1999

published by Charles Tuttle, 1999

The Vegetable Garden

by M.M. Vilmorin-Andrieux

published by Ten Speed Press

Excellent descriptions of the varieties and gardening techniques of the late 19th century in this reprint of the 1885 classic. Relevant to today.

 

 

Specific Crops

 

Smith and Hawken 100 Heirloom Tomatoes for the Garden

by Carolyn J Male

Workman Publishing Co., 1999

 

Heirloom Herbs: Using Old-Fashioned Herbs in Garden Recipes and Decorations

by Mary Forsell

Random House Canada, 1990

 

Garden Seed Inventory

by Kent Whealey, 5th edition, 1999

Seed Saver Publications, Decorah, Iowa

An inventory of seed catalogues listing all non-hybrid vegetable seeds still available in the U.S. and Canada

Available at www.seedsavers.org. or 563-382-5990

 

Fruit, Berry and Nut Inventory, 3rd edition, 2001

Seed Saver Publications, Decorah, Iowa

sourcebook for all fruit, berries, and nuts available from US mail-order nurseries, 520 pages, available as above

 

Field and Garden Vegetables of America

by Fearing Burr, reprinted 1988

The American Botanist Booksellers

Reprint of the 1865 Boston Edition of this important reference to North American varieties, first published in 1863

 

 

Seed Saving

 

How to Save Your Own Vegetable Seeds

by Seeds of Diversity, 4th edition, 1999

An illustrated booklet on saving your own seed that is of special relevance to Canadian gardeners and farmers

 

Comment produire et conserver les graines de légumes pour préserver des variétés menacées

a French translation of How to Save your own Vegetable Seeds

 

The Seed Saver's Handbook

by Cherfas, Fanton and Fanton

Grower Books, Bristol, 1996

 

Seed to Seed

by Suzanne Ashworth, Seed Saver's Exchange, Decorah, 2nd edition, 2001

extensive illustrated reference on saving over 160  vegetable seeds, 320 pages

available at www.seedsavers.org or 563-382-5990

 

Back Garden Seed Saving: Keeping our Vegetable Heritage Alive

by Susan Stickland, Eco-Logic Books, Bristol, UK, 2001, available in COG library

 

Seed Sowing and Saving

by Carole B. Turner

Storey Communications, Inc. 1997

 

Saving Seeds: The Gardener's Guide to Growing and Storing Vegetable and Flower Seeds

by Marc Rogers

Storey Communications Inc. 1991

 

The New Seed-Starters Handbook

by Nancy Bubel

Rodale Press, 1998
