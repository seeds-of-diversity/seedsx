This index of Canadian seed company catalogs shows the garden vegetable varieties available currently and during the past year.

This information is offered for free to everyone, and we don't accept advertising or charge fees to the companies.
Instead, the information is updated every year by volunteers and donations, as part of our research and monitoring of Canada's seed diversity, which drives our seed conservation priorities.

Currently indexing [[var:nTotalCultivars]] varieties from [[var:nTotalSources]] Canadian seed companies.

Our Open Data Policy

This information is collected by Seeds of Diversity, and is available for you to download and use as you wish at no cost and with no restrictions. We appreciate acknowledgement to Seeds of Diversity Canada.

As-is Data: We try very hard to ensure the accuracy of this information, but we offer it as-is with no guarantees. We welcome corrections and additions.

Need something else? If these links don't give you exactly what you're looking for, just ask. We're glad to help our community of seed diversity researchers and conservators.
